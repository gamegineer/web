Overview

Gamegineer provides a Java-based development framework for building turn-based games of arbitrary complexity ranging from Tic-Tac-Toe to Axis & Allies. The foundation of Gamegineer is the Equinox OSGi core framework, which provides other developers with opportunities to extend Gamegineer in ways not originally envisioned.

The project is currently focused on web-based correspondence play of the Axis & Allies series of strategy games.
